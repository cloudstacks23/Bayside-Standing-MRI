MRI Service

/ MRI Service

Bayside Standing MRI owns and manages the first weight-bearing magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) service in Victoria.

Read Post

This specialised imaging equipment allows patients to be scanned in a variety of positions – including standing up – unlike a tunnel MRI which has patients lying down. In this weight bearing position, we can better assess a range of issues and injuries and in some cases detect issues that won’t be detected in a lying down position.
